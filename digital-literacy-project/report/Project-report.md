## Introduction

This project was assigned as part of the Digital Literacy course to help students develop awareness about digital tools, online communication, and cyber safety. The project involved creating a digital literacy portfolio using GitHub and completing various tasks related to digital awareness, professional presence, and cybercrime prevention.

---

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

For Task 1, I created a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic using Canva. The infographic highlights important aspects of digital literacy, including safe internet practices, professional online presence, and understanding digital literacy. The design focuses on making the content visually appealing and easy to understand.

This task helped me understand how digital literacy plays an important role in students' academic and professional growth.

---

## Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

In this task, I created professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank. These platforms help in building a professional digital presence and showcasing technical skills. GitHub was used to host the project repository, LinkedIn was used for professional networking, and HackerRank was used for coding practice.

These platforms will help me improve my skills and build a professional portfolio over the next four years.

---

## Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

For Task 3, I completed a beginner challenge on HackerRank to improve coding skills. I also created a Google Form to conduct a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz. These tools help in learning collaboration and assessment techniques.

This task helped me understand how coding platforms and collaboration tools can support academic learning.

---

## Task 4 – Email Etiquette

In this task, I drafted professional emails and created a social media checklist. This task helped me understand professional communication and responsible social media usage.

Professional communication is important for academic and professional success.

---

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

For Task 5, I created a case study on UPI fraud and prepared a prevention checklist. This task helped me understand cyber threats and how to prevent them.

I learned the importance of staying alert and protecting personal information online.

---

## Conclusion

This project helped me develop digital literacy skills, professional communication, and cyber awareness. It improved my understanding of online safety and professional digital presence.
